A passing score in this course can be obtained by earning 70% of the possible points. Many times this is referred to as earning a grade of 70/100.

All assessments in this course (including the review questions) are graded and count towards your final score. The module assessments are worth one point each and the questions on the final are worth two points each. The final incorporates questions and concepts from all the modules in the course so if you concentrate and work hard on each lesson, you'll be prepared for the final exam. 

Please note that you will have 1 attempt for each question so please make sure you prepare adequately before taking the assessments. 

This course contains a final exam that is worth 82 points. The exam is composed of 41 questions valued at 2 point each.

Reminder: It is not possible to achieve a passing score in this course if you skip or fail the Final Exam. Each lesson in the course will prepare you for the final so take care to do the exercises and study the lessons.
