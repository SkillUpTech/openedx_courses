Introduce Yourself

You are not alone - there is a community of Python Learners taking this course!

You can introduce yourself to your classmates by expanding the course discussion board and responding to the "Introduce Yourself!" topic. Just like in a physical classroom, this digital classroom has the potential to provide great networking opportunities, so please also take the time to read and respond to your fellow classmates' introductions (click "Show Discussion" to get started)!

This discussion and all course discussions can be accessed any time from the "Discussion" tab above.

sample:

Hi! I am Colette from France, I am learning Python as a first programming language as part of my goal to build/publish a web app this year.
